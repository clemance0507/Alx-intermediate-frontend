What Is Article Writing?

Article writing example is the process of writing an article for a specific purpose and audience. Articles are written to discuss different subjects or topics. Articles included in publications usually contain information on current issues or events happening around the area of the writer or the publication.Writers present information in various ways, such as in an informative, or argumentative form. Basis of information written on articles may vary. Such facts may be gathered from different sources, such as eyewitness accounts, one on one interviews, and online, among others.
